# Plans & Pricing

Sylan supports **Subscription** and **PayPerCall**.

## Fields
- `accessType`: `Subscription` | `PayPerCall`
- `price`: amount in SYL
- `duration` (Subscription): time window (ms)
- `callLimit` (PPC): optional quotas
- `active`: toggle listing availability

## Fee Splits (BPS)
| Receiver | BPS |
|---|---:|
| Provider |  [example] 7000 |
| Nodes    |  [example] 2000 |
| Platform |  [example] 1000 |

> Total must equal **10,000**.
